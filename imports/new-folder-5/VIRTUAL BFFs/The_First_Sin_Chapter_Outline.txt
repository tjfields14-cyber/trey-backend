
# Chapter Outline for "The First Sin"

## Prologue: The First Sin
- **Setting:** Celestial realms and Aye (Earth-like world).
- **Event:** The Oguns, jealous of humanity, corrupt divine knowledge and sow wickedness in Aye.
- **Conflict:** A celestial war begins between Olorun's angels and the corrupted Sile Kan.

## Chapter 1: The Burning of Oduna Ketou
- **Setting:** The village of Oduna Ketou under siege.
- **Characters:** Tamaria (pregnant protagonist).
- **Event:** Tamaria struggles to survive as divine forces destroy her village.
- **Tone:** Fear, desperation, chaos.

## Chapter 2: The Angel of Death Arrives
- **Setting:** Inside Tamaria's burning home.
- **Characters:** Tamaria, Samiel.
- **Event:** Samiel confronts Tamaria, condemns humanity for its sins, and assaults her.
- **Conflict:** Physical and spiritual domination, Tamaria pleads for mercy.

## Chapter 3: Labor and Despair
- **Setting:** Ruins of Tamaria’s home.
- **Event:** Tamaria goes into labor amid chaos, injured and terrified.
- **Conflict:** The struggle between life and death, survival of her unborn child.

## Chapter 4: Tamiel’s Sacrifice
- **Setting:** Same ruins, after Samiel’s violence.
- **Characters:** Tamiel (a compassionate angel), Samiel.
- **Event:** Tamiel mourns Tamaria, attempts to save her and the child by sacrificing his essence.
- **Conflict:** Clash of ideologies between Tamiel and Samiel.

## Chapter 5: Blood and Retribution
- **Event:** Samiel murders Tamiel for his perceived betrayal.
- **Conflict:** Divine justice vs. cruelty.
- **Tone:** Tragic, sorrowful.

## Chapter 6: The Orphaned Child
- **Setting:** Aftermath of the slaughter, discovery by villagers.
- **Event:** The baby is found alive among the dead, though gravely injured.
- **Conflict:** The villagers fear the child as cursed but take him to safety.

## Chapter 7: Prophecy of the Abiku
- **Setting:** The village elders’ council and the witch Abena's dwelling.
- **Event:** The witch proclaims the child is claimed by Abiku (spirit of death) yet chooses to raise him.
- **Foreshadowing:** The boy’s future ties to divine and mortal destiny are hinted at.

---
**Overall Arc:** The prologue and opening chapters establish a dark, mythic tone, a world under celestial conflict, and the origins of a child destined to alter the fate of both divine and human realms.
